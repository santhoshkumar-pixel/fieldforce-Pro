# 🎙️ Voice AI Assistant - Quick Start Guide

## How to Use the Voice AI Assistant

### Starting Voice Input

1. **Open the Search Bar**
   - Press `Ctrl+K` or click on the search bar
   - Or click the microphone icon in the top navigation

2. **Click the Microphone Button**
   - The microphone icon appears on the right side of the search bar
   - When you click it, it will start listening for your voice
   - The icon will turn red and animate to show it's listening

3. **Speak Your Question**
   - Ask any question about the platform
   - Examples:
     - "How do I create a ticket?"
     - "Show me my tasks for today"
     - "How do I assign a device?"
     - "What is the training portal?"

### Understanding Voice Status

- 🎤 **Ready** - Microphone is ready for input, click to start
- 🎤 **Listening** (Red, animated) - Actively listening to your voice
- ⚙️ **Processing** (Violet, animated) - Converting speech to text
- 🔊 **Speaking** (Green, animated) - AI is reading the response
- ⏸️ **Paused** (Yellow) - Speech output is paused

### Voice Wave Animation

While listening, you'll see an animated wave visualization showing:
- **Real-time audio levels** from your microphone
- **Visual feedback** that the system is capturing your voice
- **Status indicator** showing the recognized text below the wave

### Getting AI Responses

1. **Automatic Response**
   - Once your speech is recognized, the search automatically runs
   - The AI matches your question to a relevant answer
   - The response appears with step-by-step instructions

2. **Auto-Speak Feature**
   - By default, the AI will read the response aloud
   - You'll see the response both on screen and hear it spoken
   - This creates a ChatGPT-like experience

### Voice Controls

After getting a response, you can:

- **🔊 Toggle Speaker** - Turn auto-speak on/off
- **⏸️ Pause** - Pause the spoken response
- **▶️ Resume** - Continue playing the paused response
- **⏹️ Stop** - Stop the speech immediately
- **🔄 Replay** - Replay the entire response again

### Advanced Features

#### 1. Voice Control Panel
- Click the **Volume icon** (next to microphone) to open full voice controls
- See detailed listening and speaking status
- Access all voice commands in one place

#### 2. Continuous Listening
- Click **"Enable Continuous Listening"** to keep microphone active
- Useful for asking multiple questions in sequence
- Click the microphone button to disable

#### 3. Recognized Text Display
- See your spoken words in real-time
- Helps verify the system understood correctly
- Appears in the listening text box

#### 4. Voice Response Display
- Currently speaking text shown in the response area
- Helps you follow along with the AI
- Updates as the speech progresses

## Troubleshooting

### "Voice search not supported"
- Your browser doesn't support Web Speech API
- Try using Chrome, Edge, or a Chromium-based browser

### Microphone not working
- Check your browser's permissions
  - Click the lock icon in the address bar
  - Allow microphone access
- Restart your browser
- Check system volume is not muted

### Speech not being recognized
- Speak more clearly and at normal pace
- Move closer to your microphone
- Ensure background noise is minimal
- Try saying the question differently

### Not hearing voice response
- Check your system volume is turned up
- Make sure speakers/headphones are connected
- Verify auto-speak is enabled (speaker icon is on)
- Try clicking the "Replay" button

### Wave animation not showing
- Ensure browser has microphone permissions
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Check browser console for errors

## Tips & Tricks

1. **Natural Language** - You don't need to ask in a specific format. Speak naturally!
   - ✅ "How can I create tickets?"
   - ✅ "I want to add a new device, what do I do?"
   - ✅ "Show me my tasks"

2. **Use Keywords** - Include main keywords for better accuracy
   - "ticket" not "that thing with the task"
   - "device" not "that IoT sensor"
   - "attendance" not "the punch in system"

3. **Pronunciation Variations** - The system handles variations
   - "technician" or "technician" (different accents)
   - "device" or "divice" (accidental mispronunciation)

4. **Multi-word Queries** - You can ask complex questions
   - "Show me all critical tickets for today"
   - "How do I assign a device to a technician?"

5. **Role-Based Responses** - Answers adapt to your role
   - Admin gets different options than Technicians
   - Managers see team-related answers
   - Technicians see personal scope answers

6. **Continuous Workflow** - Enable continuous listening for multiple questions
   - Ask first question → Get answer
   - Ask second question → Get answer
   - No need to click microphone each time

## Privacy & Security

- Voice is processed locally in your browser
- No audio recordings are stored
- Speech-to-text uses browser's native APIs
- Text-to-speech uses browser's native APIs
- All data stays within the application

## Accessibility Features

- 🎤 Works with screen readers

## Performance Notes

- Voice recognition works best with 20-30 seconds of speech
- Wave animation is GPU-accelerated for smooth performance
- Minimum requirements: Modern browser with microphone
- Works on laptops, desktops, and mobile devices with microphones
